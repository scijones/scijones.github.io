Hey, I'm Steven J. Jones and I'm a grad student at the University of Michigan in John Laird's Soar Group.

Most of my work amounts to adding subsymbolic mechanisms to the Soar Cognitive Architecture.

I've implemented spreading activation, base-level inhibition, and a few other bits in Soar. Recently, I've been focusing on implementation of event cognition functionality. My general underlying focus is on implementing subsymbolic mechanisms which provide new functionality to agents by making different types of knowledge more readily available.
